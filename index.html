About the Artist

Frank Ogbada

Frank Ogbada is an intelligent Igbo man from Nigeria, known for his exceptional talent in pencil and charcoal art. His unique style and attention to detail have captivated art enthusiasts around the world. With a deep passion for portraying emotions through art, Frank's work reflects the beauty and complexity of human experiences. Each piece tells a story, inviting viewers to explore the depths of their own emotions.

Our inspiration

"Ọ bụla asụsụ mmanụ nke ukwuu, ndị nwe nso nke omenala. Na nkịta mmụọ na-enweghị onwe ya, a gaghị enye ya ya. Mana na oge ọ bụla, ebe ndị ọzọ agagharịa, onye dị n'aka ahụ gaghị aka ya gbalịa. Na ndụ a, ọ bụla asụsụ mmanụ nke ukwuu, n'elu ọka ndịrị dị n'aka mụ, nke a ga-ekwu mmadụ na nkịta ukwuu dị n'ahụ ya."


Translation: "Art is like a mighty pencil and charcoal, possessing sacred powers. In the hands of a skilled artist, it brings forth wonders. But in the hands of another, it remains untouched. In this life, art is like a mighty pencil and charcoal, within the grasp of my hands, that will speak beauty and grandeur into its existence."

- Frank Ogbada
